== Installation ==


**Quick and Easy Installation**

1. Go to your WordPress Dashboard.
2. Navigate to Plugins > Add New.
3. Search for "Wing Popup".
4. Click on Install, then Activate the plugin.
That's it! Wing Popup is now activated on your site.


**Manual Installation**

1. Download the Wing Popup WordPress Plugin.
2. Extract the zip file.
3. Upload the contents to the wp-content/plugins/ directory of your WordPress installation.
4. Activate the plugin from the Plugins page.



== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= 1. How many popups can I create? =

There's no limit! Wing Popup allows you to create an unlimited number of popups, giving you complete flexibility to tailor your website's engagement strategy.

= 2. Can I have multiple popups on a single page? =

Absolutely! Wing Popup empowers you to create multiple popups per page. You can leverage our advanced trigger settings to define when each popup appears, ensuring a seamless user experience.

= 3. How do I control when a popup opens? =

Wing Popup offers a variety of "Trigger Events" that let you customize popup behavior. You can choose to display popups on page load, upon clicking specific elements, or even based on user scroll depth (coming soon!).

= 4. Can I set a delay before a popup appears? =

Yes, you can! Wing Popup provides a "Popup Delay" setting that allows you to control the exact time a popup appears after the trigger event is activated. This gives you granular control over your visitor's experience.

= 5. Can I trigger a popup by clicking a custom element? =

Yes indeed! Wing Popup allows you to attach any popup to open upon clicking specific elements on your website. This lets you create highly targeted popups that appear in a relevant context for your visitors.

= 6. Can I target popups to appear only on specific pages? =

Wing Popup goes beyond basic targeting. Our "Advanced Display Rules" allow you to precisely control where your popups appear. You can choose to show or hide popups on specific pages, custom posts, or even custom taxonomies, ensuring your messages reach the most relevant audience.
